About

Hi, I'm Colin, and I'm a bitworker from upstate New York.

I'm passionate about code and technology. I started Q BitWorks Inc to offer software consulting and engineering. With 20 years experience and growing, I have worked with dozens of programming languages, hundreds of frameworks, and companies large and small. I can advise you on the best tools for the job and bring your ideas from conception to deployment and beyond.

I have extensive experience writing web applications, reverse engineering both software and hardware, designing user interfaces and experiences, and much more. I can usually pick up a new language or framework in about two weeks and I am especially excited to take on a challenge.

When I'm not writing code, you can find me creating art, developing new recipes, hiking, and playing video games.

Got a challenge for me? Let's talk!
